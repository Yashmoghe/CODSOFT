A.P.J.Abdul Kalam

Visionary leader: Dr. Kalam was a visionary leader who had a clear vision for India's future. He believed that India could be a great nation in science and technology, and he worked tirelessly to make that vision a reality.
 
Humble and down-to-earth: Dr. Kalam was known for his humility and down-to-earth nature. He was always approachable and willing to listen to others. He was also a great teacher and motivator, and he inspired many people to achieve their dreams.
 
Hardworking and dedicated: Dr. Kalam was a hardworking and dedicated individual. He was always willing to put in the extra effort to get the job done. He was also a perfectionist, and he always strived for excellence.
 
Inspiring and motivational: Dr. Kalam was an inspiring and motivational speaker. He had a way of making people believe in themselves and their dreams. He also had a great sense of humor, and he often used humor to make his points.
 
Patriotic and selfless: Dr. Kalam was a patriotic and selfless individual. He dedicated his life to serving India, and he never sought personal gain. He was a true inspiration to all Indians.

some specific examples of his qualities in action

As the Scientific Adviser to the Defence Minister, he played a key role in the development of India's nuclear and missile programs.
 
As the President of India, he promoted the use of science and technology for the benefit of the common man.
 
He wrote several books, including "Wings of Fire" and "My Journey". These books are inspiring stories of his life and work.
 
He gave several motivational speeches to students and young people. These speeches inspired them to dream big and achieve their goals.
